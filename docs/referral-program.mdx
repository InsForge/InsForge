---
title: "Referral program: invite customers and earn credits"
description: "Share your InsForge referral link to invite new customers, earn billing credits on their payments, and apply accumulated credits to future invoices."
---

Share your organization's referral link to invite new customers to InsForge. Referred customers receive a $5 credit at sign-up, and your organization earns referral credits after an eligible referral makes a successful payment.

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your Referral page">
    In the InsForge dashboard, select your organization and open its **Referral** page.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy or customize your referral link">
    Copy your organization's referral link, or customize its code before sharing it.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Invite a new customer">
    Share the link with someone who is new to InsForge. They must sign up through the valid link for the referral to be attributed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Earn credits after their first payment">
    The referred customer receives their sign-up credit at sign-up. After the customer makes a successful paid subscription payment, your organization receives its referral rewards.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Rewards

| Who | Reward |
| --- | --- |
| Referred customer | $5 in credit at sign-up, automatically applied to their first paid invoice. |
| Referring organization | $10 in credit after the referred customer's first successful payment. |
| Referring organization | Credits equal to 20% of the referred customer's paid subscription invoices for 12 months from their first payment. |

## Rules

- The referral must be a new user who signs up through a valid referral link.
- Self-referrals are not eligible.
- The referring organization earns no $10 bonus or commission until the referred customer makes a successful payment.
- Commission credits tied to refunded or disputed invoices can be reversed.

## Use your credits

Referral credits belong to the organization that earned them. They never expire, accumulate in the organization's **Billing & Credits** balance, and automatically apply to future invoices.
